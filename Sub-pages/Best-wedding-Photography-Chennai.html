    Now let’s dive into the list of pre wedding photoshoot locations in Chennai.

    • Mahabalipuram Shore Temple & Beach: It is a place to capture some epic portraits. The nearby beach gives us more options for couple photoshoots, its like we get 2 amazing locations in a single place, so we can efficiently use the time and capture more couple shots.

    • Secret Garden Shooting Place – Thiruvanmiyur. If you are looking for an amazing couple photoshoot location within the city limits, then The Secret Garden is the best option you have got. They have some amazing photoshoot sets and props, so we can get maximum shots in the stipulated time frame

    • Studio 24, ECR (Near Mayajaal) offers cinematic couple photoshoot sets & props in a single location. They have 40+ different photoshoot sets, which gives so much variety during the couple photoshoots. This is currently the trending couple photoshoot location in chennai.

    • VGP Golden Beach Resort VGP has been a household name for all Chennai people for ages now.we must have visited VGP universal kingdom and VGP beach at least once in our lifetime. They also have a beach resort for couple photoshoots.

    • Intercontinental Beach Resort Situated along the scenic East Coast Road ECR, Intercontinental Chennai Mahabalipuram Resort embodies history of Coromandel Coast. Tucked away between casuarina groves the Beach Resort boasts of 15000 Sq feet garden space with private beach making it an ideal choice for Couple Photoshoots.

    • DakshinaChitra has a collection of 18 authentic historical houses with contextual exhibitions in each house, which makes it a perfect location for traditional couple photoshoot sessions.

    • Hudson resort is like a hidden gem of a couple photoshoot location in Chennai. The property is styled in Venice theme and offers amazing photoshoot opportunities.

    • Chennai Port is an offbeat photoshoot location but you need prior permission and approval for photoshoots.
