    So, if you are looking to have an amazing pre-wedding shoot in Kerala, we are here to help you out. Amidst the sandy beaches, the sparkling sunsets and the breath-taking backwaters, Kerala has a couple spots to offer you for a picture-perfect shoot


    • Tea plantations and Muthirappuzhayar River in hills near Munnar, Kerala, India

    • A scenic view of boats under a blue sky in backwaters situated in Allepey town

    • Bekal fort at Kasargod can act as a gorgeous backdrop for your pre-wedding shoots. Standing on the shore of the Arabian Sea, this fort with red walls with waves crashing into it gives stunning momentum to your photos.

    • Kumarakom: With canals everywhere, it is washed over with the scenic view of the luscious green backwaters. Surrounding these backwaters you will find rich vegetation as well as paddy fields. These make for a very lively background for your pre-wedding shoot.

    • Thrissur is also home to many exotic beaches and waterfalls which makes it one of the best places for your pre-wedding shoot in Kerala.

    • Kochi also known as Cochin, this city is brimming with paddy fields, backwaters, swaying palms, white sands, and the like. It is a charming little part of Kerala, which is versatile and lively, making it a very likely choice for a perfect location for your pre-wedding shoot.

    • With waters, hills, greens, plantations and even some pineapple farms, Kava, near Palakkad is such a wonderful place for you to go and get your pictures clicked with your fiance. Ottapalam is also an option if you are looking for a quiet and calm little spot brimming with natural beauty. You could also check out the Pothundi Dam if that suits your fancy.

    • Wyanad : Just as scenic as Munnar, Wayanad is overflowing with jaw-droppingly stunning paddy fields, sweeping views and exotic resorts. It’s a cosy little place for you to check out with natural backgrounds to work well for your pre-wedding shoot in Kerala.

    • Ashtamudi Lake is best visited atop a houseboat that takes you along the eye-catching sceneries this lake has to offer. It’s a fairly well-connected location, easy to reach with minimal effort. With the blue skies and the bluer waters, Ashtamudi lake is a serene sight to see. If you are a fan of the cool tones, being contrasted by soft greens of the palm trees, this is the spot for you.

    • Wayanad : Just as scenic as Munnar, Wayanad is overflowing with jaw-droppingly stunning paddy fields, sweeping views and exotic resorts. It’s a cosy little place for you to check out with natural backgrounds to work well for your pre-wedding shoot in Kerala.

    • If you are looking for white sands, with an azure sea behind them, Kovalam is the place you should go. If warm tones soothe your soul, remember to plan your shoot for the evening, because Kovalam has the most picturesque sunset with dusty skies with sprinkles of color everywhere. All in all, this city is an ideal choice for the location for your pre-wedding shoot in Kerala
